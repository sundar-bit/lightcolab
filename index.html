The Studio

A practice built on the belief that good light is felt, not just seen.

Light Colab is an independent architectural lighting design firm, with studios in Chennai and Bangalore. For over a decade we have practised lighting design across national and international projects — more than 250 to date, spanning every kind of space and genre.

Our philosophy is to provide solutions that are eminent, balanced, and impressive, yet sustainable. We stay project-centric, holding a careful equipoise between stakeholders, budgets, timelines and commitments on every brief.

Staying current with market trends and product developments is vital to how we work — it lets us match each design solution to the latest industry technology, from concept through commissioning.

How we work

Concept to hand over.

A structured six-stage process that carries every project from first idea to a commissioned, working installation.

01

Concept Design

Reading the architecture and intent, and shaping the lighting idea — mood, layers and design language for the space.

02

Schematic Design

Translating the concept into lighting layouts, zones and a preliminary fixture strategy for each area.

03

Detailed Design

Fixture selection, lux-level calculations, the controls scheme and full technical documentation.

04

Tendering

Specifications, BOQ and tender support so the project team can procure the right products and vendors.

05

Site Progress

Coordination, installation support and on-site reviews to ensure the design is realised as intended.

06

Hand Over

Focusing, scene-setting and controls commissioning — handing over a working, documented installation.
